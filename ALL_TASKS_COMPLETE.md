## 🎨 DESIGN HIGHLIGHTS

### Color Palette
- **Primary Text**: `var(--text-primary)` (#f4f4ff)
- **Secondary Text**: `var(--text-secondary)` (#b8b8cc)
- **Accent Blue**: `#0066ff` (primary accent)
- **Accent Cyan**: `#00b6d4` (hover, active states)
- **Accent Purple**: `#7c3aed` (secondary accent)
- **Background**: `rgba(8, 8, 16, ...)` (dark theme)

### Glass Morphism
- **Blur**: 28px-40px depending on state
- **Opacity**: 0.45-0.88 depending on scroll
- **Border**: `rgba(255,255,255,0.1)` to `0.15`
- **Shadow**: Inset and drop shadows for depth

### Animations
- **Timing**: All use `ease-in-out` for natural motion
- **Duration**: 20-55s for imperceptible change
- **Framework**: Framer Motion with spring physics
- **Performance**: GPU-accelerated, 60fps target
