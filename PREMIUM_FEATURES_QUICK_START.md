1. Click the **🎨** button (bottom-right corner)
2. Choose your preferred accent color:
   - Blue (default)
   - Cyan (cool)
   - Purple (elegant)
   - Emerald (fresh)
   - Orange (warm)
3. Click to apply instantly
4. Your choice is automatically saved
